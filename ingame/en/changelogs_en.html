Changelog - Star Grow

Here you can find all the updates and improvements made to the game Star Grow. Stay up to date with the latest news and changes!

Version 0.1.2.0 - December 26, 2024

  • New Menu, Daily Missions: Receive missions daily, each completed mission provides Infinity Coupon rewards.
  • Conversion Button: Convert Infinity Coupons to Creation Points in the Creation menu.
  • New Creation Item, Energetic Tap: Generate Energy whenever you touch the star.
  • New Creation Item, Tap Intensity: Increase tap energy generation whenever you touch the star.
  • Improvements and Bug Fixes.

Version 0.1.1.40 - December 11, 2024

  • Modification to the ad system.
  • Translation of terms of use and privacy policy.
  • Improvements and bug fixes.

Version 0.1.1.37 - November 13, 2024

  • Bug fixes for behavior issues.
  • Improvement of the Initialization system.
  • Added News & Social menu (message balloon).

Version 0.1.0.86 - October 8, 2024

  • Initial release of the game Star Grow!
  • Basic features for star management.
